Hey there! Welcome to the Tips page. In this page itself, we linked a few pages
that are really useful that will definitely help you in making better financial choices.

The Woke Salaryman

The Woke Salaryman is a very good website for Singaporeans as most of their finance topics are related to Singapore, such as CPF, HDB and much more. Not only that, the way that they provide tips for finances are very visual representative as they are portrayed in cartoon.

Money Smart

Money smart is a really useful website that helps compare different financial products. It is really useful if you want to take up financial loan or credit card loans as they provide comparisons of different companies and allow you to do some research before deciding on one.

Money Sense

Money Sense is a Singapore Governement website where they offer different features in helping you learn more about finances such as retirement and loans.
